WHAT DO WE DO?

Short answer: we specialized in professional dog grooming, including cats and small animals, in the most relaxed environment.

From the first time you book an appointment via phone, we'd like to know a little more about your furkid and yourself. Come experience our open-concept grooming salon on your schedule. Then let our prefoessional pet stylists do the bath, brush, trim while you may take a 1-2 minutes walk to Thomas Plaza to shop or enjoy a Starbucks brew.

If you have a specific time you need your dog to be picked up, please notify us at the time of drop-off. There is no additional fee to hold your dog for you until you are able to pick up.
